Prediction

Day 1 Barrier Prediction

Our AI analyzes admission data in real-time to flag discharge barriers before they become bottlenecks.

  • Integrated with Epic and all major EMRs
  • Predicts insurance, placement, and equipment barriers
  • Risk-scores every patient on admission
  • Surfaces actionable alerts to your case management team
Automation

Autonomous Prior Authorization

Clementine compiles and submits complete prior auth packets built to maximize approvals and cut wait times by days.

  • Auto-generates complete auth packages from the patient's chart
  • Optimizes documentation to meet payer-specific approval criteria
  • Navigates and submits directly through payer portals
  • Tracks submission status so case managers don't have to follow up manually
